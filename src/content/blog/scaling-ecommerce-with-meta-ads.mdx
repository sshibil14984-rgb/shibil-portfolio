# Precision Scaling: The Meta Ads Playbook

Scaling an e-commerce brand today isn't about finding a "magic" interest group or a hidden hack. It's about **Systematic Creative Testing** and **Account Consolidation**.

In this guide, we'll break down the exact framework I use to scale brands from $10k/month to $100k/month predictably.

## 1. The Death of Granular Targeting

Gone are the days of layering 50 different interests. With the evolution of Advantage+ and Meta's AI (Lattice), the algorithm performs best when given more data.

**The Strategy:**

- Use Broad targeting (Age, Gender, Location only).
- Let the creative do the targeting.
- Consolidate your campaigns to exit the learning phase faster.

## 2. Creative is the New Targeting

Since we are using Broad targeting, your ad creative must resonate specifically with your ideal customer.

- **Hook:** Grab attention in the first 3 seconds.
- **Body:** Address the pain point and present your solution.
- **CTA:** Clear and direct action.

## 3. The Power of ROAS vs. POAS

While ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) is the standard metric, high-growth brands focus on **POAS (Profit on Ad Spend)**. Understanding your contribution margin allows you to spend more aggressively than your competitors.

### Key Takeaways:

- Simplify your account structure.
- Test at least 3-5 new creatives every week.
- Focus on first-party data for retargeting.
